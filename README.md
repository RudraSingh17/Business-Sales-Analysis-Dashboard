📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
🔍 Key Insights & Features
Sales Performance: Analyzed total revenue (214.92M) and profit (88.38M) across different time periods to identify trends and business growth.
Geographical Analysis: Used map visualization to highlight revenue contribution from different countries.
Product Analysis: Identified top-performing products like 4K Monitor and Smart Watch driving major revenue.
Category Insights: Electronics and Sports categories contribute the highest share of revenue and profit.
Profitability Analysis: Compared revenue vs profit using scatter plot to identify high-profit segments.
Trend Analysis: Visualized revenue trends over time to detect fluctuations and growth patterns.
🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI Desktop: Data visualization and dashboard creation
Power Query: Data cleaning and transformation
DAX: Created calculated measures for KPIs
📂 Dashboard Structure
🔹 Page 1: Sales Overview
KPI Cards (Revenue, Profit, Quantity, Cost)
Revenue by Category (Pie Chart)
Revenue by Product & Subcategory (Bar Chart)
Revenue by Country (Map)
Revenue vs Profit (Scatter Plot)
Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
🔹 Page 2: Detailed Analysis
Top Products Table
Sales Quantity Distribution (Donut Chart)
Sales Funnel by Category
Profit by Category (Treemap)
Profit Contribution (Waterfall Chart)
Average Profit Margin KPI
📌 How to Use
Download the .pbix file
Open it in Power BI Desktop
Use slicers (Month, Year, Country) to interact with the dashboard
Explore insights across different visuals
🎯 Conclusion

This dashboard provides a complete view of sales performance and helps in making data-driven decisions by identifying trends, top products, and profitable categories.
